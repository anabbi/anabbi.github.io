Hello! Thanks for visiting my page!

I am Arash. I am a registered pharmacist and a cancer biologist with post-doctoral experience in computational genomics. I am interested in predictive biomarkers and novel targets in cancer immunotherapy, with special focus on pediatric solid tumours. I am most interested in using single-cell and spatial transcriptomics to guide target discovery.
